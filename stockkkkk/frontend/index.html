How It Works

Step 1

Input Your Investment Profile

Enter your capital, choose your risk tolerance and investment horizon. Optionally specify preferred stocks.

1
2
Step 2

Data Fetch & LSTM Training

Backend fetches 2 years of OHLCV data from Yahoo Finance, scales it with MinMaxScaler, and trains (or loads) an LSTM model.

Step 3

Risk Analysis & Scoring

Sharpe Ratio, Volatility, Max Drawdown, and VaR are computed. Each stock receives a risk classification and a composite score.

3
4
Step 4

Portfolio Allocation

Top-ranked stocks are selected and capital is distributed using inverse-volatility weighting — lower volatility gets more allocation.
